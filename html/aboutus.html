About Us

Willify is a music streaming service that offers access to music tracks, podcasts, and other media through a subscription model. Willify gets its content from major record labels and independent artists, and pays royalties for streamed music. As of March 2018, Willify had 159 million active users and a library of 35 million songs, and is used in 61 countries.

Our Founders

Daniel Ek
Martin Lorentzon

Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon founded Willify in Stockholm, Sweden in 2000. Willify made its debut in 2006 in Scandinavia, the United Kingdom, France, and Spain.
